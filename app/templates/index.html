About our founder:

Jon Curtis is the founder of our Christian men's fraternity for American business owners. Drawing on his personal journey of overcoming homelessness, Jon's mission is to uplift fellow brothers in faith and business. He strives to lead by example, both in his professional pursuits and charitable work, with a deep commitment to helping others and strengthening his community through the teachings of Jesus Christ.
