Our Mission

Our mission at the New Christian Order is to create a network of Christians dedicated to building the kingdom and lifting each other up. NCO is a fraternal organization based on the lodge system. We provide benefits to our members like spiritual support, networking events, expert-led seminars, and of course, brotherhood.

We currently have lodges in Austin, Texas; Bend, Oregon; and Wilkes-barre, Pennsylvania. If you are interested in joining (even if you live in a different area), sign up on our Join page and one of our head marshalls will reach out to you.
